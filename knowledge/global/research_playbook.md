# Research Playbook

## Default Procedure

1. Preserve the user's original question as the root artifact.
2. Split the research into independent angles.
3. Search in parallel across providers.
4. Extract evidence into source artifacts.
5. Convert evidence into precise claims.
6. Challenge claims with missing-evidence and contradiction questions.
7. Mine insights from cross-source tension.
8. Audit citations before writing the report.
